AI Privacy Forensic Auditor

A modular forensic pipeline that asks a sharp question: how much does your model remember about its training data? Built around Membership Inference Attacks, it systematically probes the privacy boundaries of ML classifiers.

  • Preprocessing engine for 48,000+ census records — automated cleaning, PCA-based multivariate analysis
  • Reliability module measuring stability across 10+ stratified runs, 95% confidence intervals and calibration curves
  • Shadow Model training in integration — adversarial simulation to extract training data signatures
  • Full modular pipeline design: swap any component independently without breaking the audit chain
